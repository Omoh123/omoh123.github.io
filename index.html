About

History major, brand strategist by proof of work.

I didn't set out to work in marketing. I studied History at the University of Benin, which turned out to be its own kind of training: reading context, following a thread to its source, understanding why people believe what they believe before you ask them to believe something new. I use that instinct on every brand I touch.

Since late 2025, I've gone from supporting brand operations to owning strategy end-to-end — content direction, campaign scripting, creator pipelines, and the automation that holds it all together. I have converted this experience into value for consumer brands that need their content to do more than perform.

I was promoted within months of starting, not because I was the loudest in the room, but because the work moved numbers.

I'm currently looking for a role with a team that wants a strategist who thinks in outcomes: leads, conversions, revenue, not just content calendars.
